About Luminary

Education that adapts to you

We founded Luminary because learning should never feel like a one-size-fits-all experience. Our AI engine analyzes your background, goals, and learning rhythm to build a curriculum that's genuinely yours.

Learner-first design — every feature is built around the person learning, not the content creator.
Real-world outcomes — we measure success by what you can do after, not by hours watched.
Radical accessibility — quality education at a price that doesn't require a scholarship.
